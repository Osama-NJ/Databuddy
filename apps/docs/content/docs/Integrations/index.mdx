## Quick Setup Guide

**1. Choose Your Platform**
Select the integration that matches your tech stack from the options above.

**2. Get Your Client ID**
Find your unique client ID in your [Databuddy dashboard](https://app.databuddy.cc/settings).

**3. Follow Integration Steps**
Each integration has specific setup instructions tailored to that platform.

**4. Verify Tracking**
Check your dashboard for real-time data after installation.
